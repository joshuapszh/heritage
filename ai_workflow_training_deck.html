First Principles of NotebookLM

Source-Grounded AI

It is locked only to the PDFs and documents you upload. It builds solely from your reality.

Zero Hallucinations

It cannot pull unverified text or fake facts from the open internet. Airtight compliance.

Contextual Intelligence

Calibrates perfectly for a 9-year-old or an IGCSE candidate based entirely on your prompt.

Phase Three

Building The Hub

Google Classroom Deployment

BLOCK 3

Structure & Deploy

Create Class & Instant Enroll

Project your 6-character Class Code. Peers click "Join Class" for frictionless enrollment.

Organize with Topics

Go to Classwork > Create > Topic. Name it after your unit (vital for primary student navigation).

Post Materials

Create Material. Upload your polished PowerPoint and AI Lesson Plan under your new Topic.

BLOCK 3

Connecting AI to Assessment

The Form

Create a Quiz Assignment. Paste your 4 NotebookLM questions into the Blank Form and set the Answer Key.

The Rubric

Back in Classroom, click + Rubric. Build a simple 2-point scale for the short answer (2=Complete, 1=Partial, 0=Missing).
